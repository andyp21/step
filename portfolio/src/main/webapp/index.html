"Whenever people ask to describe myself, I often get lost in the ambiguity of the question. I typically say I am a Princeton engineering undergrad, leaving the rest of me submerged beneath the surface of a soft smile. I'm a Jamaican? - I might add. A millenial? Someone who aspires to be more than he is?

The truth is, a couple of characters on a screen could never capture the lives we lead when we put down the keyboard. The evenings we spent training to the beat of our swim coach's commands, or the summers when we tore apart computers in a garage as we searched for the magic that our parents ensured us was there."
